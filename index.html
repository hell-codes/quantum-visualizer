The Schrödinger Equation

iħ ∂ψ/∂t = −(ħ² / 2m) ∇²ψ + Vψ

The Schrödinger equation describes how quantum particles behave as waves. The wavefunction ψ(x,t) contains the probability distribution of a particle.
